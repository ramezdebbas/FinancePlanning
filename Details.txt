The Finance and Planning:
Description:
The Finance & Planning is an app having the various useable collection of calculators such as Basic, Mortgage, Auto Loan, Body Mass Index, Credit Card Pay Off, Idea Weight, Dating Age, Simple Loans & Simple Savings. You can use them and make your financial life very easy. 

Features:
•	Various Types of Calculators.
•	Basic Calculator.
•	Mortgage Calculator.
•	Auto Loan Calculator.
•	Credit Card Pay Off Calculator.
•	Body Mass Index Calculator.
•	Idea Weight Calculator.
•	Dating Age Calculator.
•	Simple Loans Calculator.
•	Simple Savings Calculator.
•	User Interaction & Support.
•	Task to do. 
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
